## Core Features

### 1. Organized Conversations
**Headline:** Clarity amidst the chaos.
**Copy:** Keep your team synchronized with structured channels for every project and topic.
*   **Public & Private Channels:** Flexible spaces for team-wide announcements or sensitive discussions.
*   **Threads:** Dive deep into topics without cluttering the main feed. Slide-over thread panels keep context visible.
*   **Direct Messages:** Fast, private 1:1 or group conversations.

### 2. Hazel Connect
**Headline:** Collaborate beyond borders.
**Copy:** Break down silos by securely connecting with partners, agencies, and customers directly within Hazel.
*   **Shared Channels:** Work side-by-side with external teams in real-time.
*   **Secure Access:** Granular permissions ensure external guests only see what they need to.
*   **Zero Context Switching:** Stop juggling emails and multiple guest accounts.

### 3. A Powerful Editor
**Headline:** Express yourself clearly.
**Copy:** A rich text editor built for developers and creators.
*   **Markdown Support:** Format text naturally as you type.
*   **Code Blocks:** Syntax highlighting for sharing snippets.
*   **Rich Media:** Drag-and-drop file uploads, image previews, and seamless link embeds.

### 4. Integrated Workflows
**Headline:** Your central command center.
**Copy:** Bring your favorite tools directly into your conversation stream.
*   **Project Management:** Sync issues and updates from **Linear**.
*   **Scheduling:** Book meetings instantly via **Cal.com**.
*   **Collaboration:** Preview and share **Google Docs** without leaving the chat.
*   **And More:** Infinite possibilities with our open integration platform.

### 5. Speed & Efficiency
**Headline:** Built for flow state.
**Copy:** Navigate your entire workspace without lifting your fingers from the keyboard.
*   **Command Palette (Cmd+K):** Instantly jump between channels, find teammates, or tweak settings.
*   **Real-Time:** Sub-millisecond message delivery. See who's typing and who's online instantly.

### 6. Developer First
**Headline:** Extensible by design.
**Copy:** Hazel isn't just a chat app; it's a platform.
*   **Bot Ecosystem:** Automate workflows with a robust Bot SDK.
*   **Webhooks:** React to events in real-time.
*   **Open Source:** Inspect the code, contribute features, or host it yourself.
